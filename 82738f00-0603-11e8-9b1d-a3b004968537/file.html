Why Jèrriais?

The island’s postal service has been part of island life since 1794. As such, we are proud of our history and keen to promote our heritage, including our language. By clicking on the link to translate, you will be able to view our home page translated into Jerriais, which we are promoting as part of an initiative launched by the Societé Jersiaise.
